Nijat Isgandarov

Industrial/Systems Engineer

Nijat Isgandarov is an Azerbaijan Fulbright scholar pursuing a master's in industrial engineering/systems engineering at the State University of New York, Buffalo. He holds a BS in industrial engineering and an MS in engineering management. Isgandarov has 9 years of experience in the oil and gas and production industries. He gained a senior project engineer (SPE) position in 2017. As an SPE, he completed several projects. Nijat coordinated all aspects of the project from initiation to closeout. Moreover, he was certified in project management from the American Technical University. He has advanced knowledge of the 3D Printing/Mechanical Engineering; Data Science and the following software: MySQL, PYTHON, TensorFlow (Machine Learning), Aveva E3D, PIPESIM, PRO II, AutoCAD, HYSYS, ECLIPSE. He plans to become an engineering/project manager. Additionally, he plans to obtain a Ph.D. in industrial engineering in the future.
